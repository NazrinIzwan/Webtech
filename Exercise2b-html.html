Spend time with your family and friends at Sunway Lagoon!

Sunway Lagoon is a popular amusement park located in Malaysia. It is divided into three different themed zones, each offering a unique experience.


Attractions

Sunway Lagoon is a thrill-seeker's paradise, with an abundance of exciting rides, attractions, and experiences to delight visitors of all ages. This amusement park is the ultimate destination for anyone looking for an adrenaline rush, and it boasts some of the most exhilarating rides in the world! From the heart-pumping excitement of the Extreme Park to the wet and wild adventures of the Water Park, you'll never be short of ways to get your pulse racing. There are many exciting types of attractions here in Sunway Lagoon, check it out!


Amusement Park Water Park Wildlife Park


  • Amusement Park

    • Amusement Park includes various rides and attractions such as roller coasters, a pirate ship, a carousel, bumper cars, and a Ferris wheel. You can challenge yourself on the world's largest slingshot, the G-Force X, or experience the thrill of a 360-degree loop on the pirate ship, the Pirate's Revenge. If you're feeling brave, you can even bungee jump from a height of 22 meters at the Bungee Jump Zone!




  • Water Park

    • Water Park is home to some of the most thrilling water rides you'll ever experience. This zone features various water rides, including water slides, a wave pool, a lazy river, and a surf pool.




  • Wildlife Park

    • The Wildlife Park at Sunway Lagoon is a must-visit attraction for animal lovers of all ages. Here, you'll have the opportunity to get up close and personal with a wide variety of exotic creatures from around the world, all in a safe and controlled environment. This zone is home to over 150 species of animals, including tigers, lions, and orangutans.
